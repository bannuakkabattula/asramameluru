NCHPE 2024 Pre-Conference Workshops: A Feast of Learning!

Get ready for an exciting lineup of pre-conference workshops at NCHPE 2024! We received a record-breaking 45 proposals, making it incredibly difficult to choose the best ones. Do not worry, we have a diverse selection to cater to everyone's interests. We would like to limit the numbers to 30 participants per workshop, so hurry and register!!!!

Here's what awaits you


  • Internationally Acclaimed Experts: (PCW1 & PCW9) Learn from renowned guest faculty from abroad and gain valuable insights beyond your borders. You can choose only one workshop from this category.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration: (PCW2-8 & PCW10-17) Dive deep into multi-institutional workshops designed by faculty from various disciplines. These workshops address diverse areas of HP Education, from practical teaching techniques to personal and professional development, and even glimpses into future educational trends!
  • Student Spotlight: (PCWS1, 2 & 4) Undergraduate and postgraduate stu- dents, there are workshops dedicated just for you!
  • Boost Your Research Skills: (PCWS-3) Calling all undergraduate medical students! This unique workshop, led by experienced near-peer leaders, equips you with research and publication skills – a valuable asset considering the NMC's definition of Indian Medical Graduates (IMGs) as researchers.
  • Discipline-Specific Focus: (PCW DS-1 & DS-2) AYUSH, Physiotherapy, and Dentistry faculty members, connect with colleagues from your discipline! Dis- cuss curriculum reform challenges and develop strategies to strengthen your programs.
  • The Best of Both Worlds: (PCWH-1 & 2) Can't attend NCHPE in person? No worries!

Our hybrid workshops allow you to participate virtually from anywhere. A Chance for New Voices:
We've taken a leap of faith by including workshops from the next generation of educators. Register for these sessions and empower them to make their mark! Your participation will make a real difference.

A Word to Rejected Applicants:
We were simply helpless and had turn down your applications with heavy hearts. We understand your disappointment if your proposal wasn't chosen. The selection process was incredibly competitive. However, we encourage you to actively participate in all the scientific events at NCHPE 2024.


Get ready for an enriching learning experience! We wish you a happy work- shopping experience!
